MAINTENANCE

At Optimo our passion for cleaning and maintenance ensures that we provide a consistent customer-focused service.

We are an honest family run business that has been servicing Cardiff and the surrounding areas for many years. Over these years we have built trust by providing a consistent reliable cleaning experience. This is why many of our clients continue to choose Optimo for their home or workplace.

With more and more customers signing up for our cleaning and maintenance services every day, its clear recommendations have spread through friends and family of our existing customers, which we find to be the best testimony of our quality and reliability.

We offer personalised and customisable services ranging from one off domestic deep cleaning to regular maintenance of commercial properties. Our in house maintenance team offer a bespoke service that can take away the headache any issue including Electrical, Plumbing, Handyman, Windows & Doors, Painting, Carpentry & much more.
